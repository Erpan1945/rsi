A Brief Introduciton to Raja Ampat

Located in the Raja Ampat Regency, West Papua, the Raja Ampat Archipelago spans an impressive 4.6 million hectares. This area is home to 75% of the world's marine species, truly earning its reputation as an underwater paradise. Without a doubt, Raja Ampat is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Papua.
